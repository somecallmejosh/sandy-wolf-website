Essentially, I help people who struggle with the “stuff of Life”; people and families who:

  • feel “lost” or disconnected
  • feel frustrated and disappointed in the outcomes of their lives
  • have disruptive despair or anxiety
  • want to improve their self-concept
  • want to improve the quality of their relationships
  • are parents struggling to understand and parent their children
  • are in crisis
  • are couples wanting to strengthen their bond
  • are adolescents struggling in school, at home or with friends
  • are couples “on the brink” of divorce and uncertain whether their marriage can be saved
  • want to participate in a divorce process with integrity, health and respect
